A placeholder that for now can be summed with the following analytics mantra:

  • Every problem has knowable solutions.
  • Analytics require a code of ethics.
  • A number by itself signifies nothing.
  • All assumptions are wrong but some are useful.†
  • Errors are your friends.

†Credit to two brilliant minds. Box's original line is "All models are wrong but some are useful." One of my dear friends and former colleague provided this re-scripting.
